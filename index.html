Love For Environment
Be Creative!

I have always been concerned about the Environment. In School, I made posters encouraging students to recycle and hung them up everywhere. I didn't realize at the time it was to protect the power lines, but the good intentions were there. My concern for protecting the environment(and every tree on the street) has only grown stronger as I've grown up. Humans are fully dependent on natural resources, but we continue to overfish oceans, chop down rainforests, and use harmful practices like fracking to extract oil from the earth. Every poor choice we make now will spiral out to impact future generations. I always find ways for humans to take care of the environment instead of exploiting it.
